The Ministry of Economy and Knowledge, Startups and Microenterprises and the Ministry of the Environment and Renewable Energies are organizing the

Greentech
Program

June 1st - Sep 09, 2023.

The "GreenTech program" initiative aims to develop start-up innovation for new products, uses and services aimed at protecting the environment and strengthening the green and circular economy. It will focus on the following areas

Recycling and
Waste management

Identify and promote innovative solutions to minimize waste generation and optimize waste collection, treatment and recovery processes.

Sustainable
cities

Identify and promote innovative solutions for green city projects, sustainable building materials and eco-mobility.

Renewable
energy

Identify and promote innovative solutions to boost clean and renewable energies.

Open innovation for responsible R&D

Detecting and promoting innovative solutions on behalf of large companies within the framework of open innovation. With this objective, the program aims to change the philosophy from large polluters paying to polluters investing in innovative solutions for environmental protection.

Program timeline

01-06-2023 Call for participation launch ceremony

31-07-2023 Registration deadline and selection of five projects by theme

01-08-2023 - 31-08-2023 Support and training program

02-09-2023 Pitch session and selection of winners

09-09-2023 Awards ceremony of the winner of each theme.
